Eat fresh — live healthy

Hello, welcome to Calderwood Heritage Centre, a new fantastic place where you can completely immerge your mind in the nature and forget your daily life for a while. Let us take care of you, we're really good at it, we promise!

Eat fresh food

Spend some relaxing time in our cafe, choosing among a wide variety of organic coffees, add a bit of sweetness with our special vegan cakes. Or enjoy a lunch and try our menu. Every recipe is made by our chef with our organic products to ensure that you're 100% happy. All our vegetables are fresh, organic and local.

Buy or trade a plant

We really take care of the nature. That's why at Calderwood we give you the possibility to buy or trade a plant. It's simple, you want to give away a plant, you just leave it here in our plant shelter/shop and you can buy another one with a discount. We'll take care of you plant until she's ready to be adopted again. We raise all our plants by hand at our nursery!

Play with our animals

You'll see how happy our animals are. They are raised without added hormones or antibiotics, just natural food. They are going to live here happily and joyfully. At Calderwood we don't eat our animals, they are here to share time and happiness with people.

Walk into the Nature

Calderwood is a magic place, the Nature will surround you every moment. Take your time, breathe and choose a direction. You will fill embraced by the trees and the bright green. You can choose among different paths, the wood, the sea, the archeological site by the heritage.
