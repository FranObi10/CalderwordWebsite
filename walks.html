Walks & Parks

Walks — Parks

Discover Calderwood Park and the paths around the Heritage. They are fantastic for those who want to discover the country on foot or by bike, or even along one route, in a canoe! The trails are distinctively waymarked, largely off-road and traffic free and have a range of visitor services along the way. You can enjoy the trails your own way – take the family and walk or bike a route in small sections on an afternoon or day trip, or plan a multi-day epic and tackle a trail in one go.
