Our Menu

Drink a coffe — Buy a plant

Bringing you Organic, Delicious Veg food! Happy, Wholesome Organic Juices, Cakes (including Raw Cakes), Breakfasts & Lunch. Most of our sweet treats are sugar free and when we not use unrefined/unprocessed sugar. The lovely team here works with local suppliers and small businesses to offer you the finest 100% organic ingredients which are delicious. From strong, dark roasted coffee (specially blended) to nutritional boasting juices; everything you eat, drink, smell and see has been made as naturally as possible. We believe that food should be grown and sourced in an ethical, clean and positive manner which supports our environment. All takeaway options (cups, napkins, cutlery etc.) are vegware compostable. You can put them in your food recycle bin.

We grow wild-species plants as have evolved with pollinators over millions of years, and raised from seed, as nature intended. Also heritage variety seed which has been bred open-pollinated with bees over many generations. All our plants produce pollen and nectar in return for pollination, which produce fruit and seed. Our organic seed is produced on certified organic land and is guaranteed free of pesticides, all the other seed we sell is guaranteed un-treated.
